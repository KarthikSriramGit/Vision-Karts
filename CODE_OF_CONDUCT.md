## Community Guidelines

This project aims to build a welcoming, professional space for people interested in computer vision, retail technology, and automated checkout systems.

Everyone participating in this project is expected to treat others with respect and follow the guidelines below.

### Our Standards

Examples of behavior that helps create a positive environment include:

- Being respectful of different viewpoints and experiences
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing othersâ€™ private information without explicit permission
- Any other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

### Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards of acceptable behavior and are expected to take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

Maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that do not align with these standards.

### Scope

These guidelines apply within all project spaces, including:

- GitHub issues and pull requests
- Code reviews
- Discussions and chat channels related to the project

### Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates these guidelines, please contact the maintainers at `support@vision-karts.ai`.

All reports will be reviewed and handled as promptly and fairly as possible.

### Enforcement

Project maintainers will determine the appropriate response to any reported incident, which may include:

- A request for a private or public apology
- A warning
- Temporary or permanent restrictions on participation
- Removal from the project community in severe cases

### Attribution

These guidelines are inspired by the Contributor Covenant and other open source community policies, adapted for this project.
